About Makini Primary School
Welcome to Makini Primary School, a beacon of educational excellence nestled in the heart of Kenya. Established with a vision to nurture young minds and inspire a lifelong love for learning, Makini Primary School has been a cornerstone of quality education since its inception.

At Makini Primary School, we are dedicated to providing a holistic education that goes beyond textbooks. Our mission is to cultivate a stimulating environment where academic achievement, character development, and creativity converge to shape well-rounded individuals ready to face the challenges of the future.

Makini Primary School embraces a child-centric approach to learning, recognizing the unique strengths and potential of each student. We believe in fostering curiosity, critical thinking, and a sense of responsibility , laying the foundation for a lifetime of intellectual and personal growth.
