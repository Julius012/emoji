School Fees Structure at Makini Schools

We believe in providing a world-class education that is accessible and transparent. Our school fees are structured to cover various aspects of your child's educational journey, ensuring a holistic learning experience. Below is a breakdown of the school fees for the academic year 2024.

1. Tuition Fees:

  • Class 1 to 3: KES 1000
  • Class 4 to 6: KES 2000
  • Class 7 to 8: KES 4000

2. Additional Costs:

  • Uniform: KES 3500
  • Books and Stationery: KES 4000
  • Extracurricular Activities: KES 2000
  • Examination Fees: KES 600

3. Payment Options:

Fees can be paid annually, per term, or in convenient installments. Payment can be made through bank transfers, mobile money, or in-person at the school's finance office.

4. Additional Information:

All fees are due by the end of the term. Late payments may incur a penalty of 3%. For any concerns or queries regarding school fees, please contact our finance office at 0742679432.

5. Financial Aid:

Makini Schools is committed to providing financial aid to deserving students. Families facing financial constraints are encouraged to explore our financial aid programs.

We understand the importance of financial planning for your child's education. If you have further questions or need assistance, feel free to reach out to our finance office.

Thank you for choosing Makini Schools for your child's educational journey!
